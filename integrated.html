Land Transportation

EMT provides a wide range of trucks meeting all transport requirements within Egypt as well as North Africa & Middle East Region. Our state of the art fleet includes, trailers, flatbeds and refer trucks. We provide guaranteed daily services to all major cities in the region.

EMT has many years of experience in moving all types of manufactured goods, consumable, bulk liquids, chemicals, foodstuffs, raw materials and project cargo. Our professional services is derived from our constant recognition of the needs of our customers and our ability to offer them the suitable transport solution.

EMT's team of professional and fully dedicated trucking managers are on call 24/7 and our trucking supervisors are always present at main loading sites and boarder points overcoming any obstacles to ensure on-time deliveries.

Our Land Transportation services includes:

  • Container Transport.
  • Bulk & Break Bulk Transport.
  • Reefer Transport.
  • Liquid Transport.
  • Over size over weight cargo transport.
  • Furniture & H.H. goods Transport.

Our Customs Clearance services include:

Fresh Produce & Reefer cargo Transport

EMT is one of the leading companies in Egypt and the MENA region specialized in the field of fresh produce and reefer cargo handling. Our professional team have many years experience in the handling of citrus, onions, potatoes, frozen vegetables, dairy products, ... etc.

EMT provides all types of required trucks and trailers according to the nature of the transported goods starting from regular flat bed trucks, Side walls and 13.6 m Thermo king reefer trailer units. Our good reputation in this field was derived from our long years experience and our constant focus on our customer's needs for swift and on-time deliveries for such sensitive products with a limited shelf life.

EMT overland reefer trailer services is not only limited to Egypt, North Africa and Arabian Gulf Countries but also extends all the way to Europe and UK through our network of specialized and reliable overseas partners and our long term relations with Ro-Ro vessel owners operating at both Red Sea and Mediterranean region.

Our Perishable cargo services include:

  • Modern Fleet of flat bed, side walls and 13.6 m Thermo King reefer trailers.
  • Professional handling of frozen products, fresh vegetables & highly sensitive fruits.
  • 24/7 presence by our customs brokers and supervisors at all Egyptian gateways and boarder points.
  • Employed customs brokers and supervisors escorting customs delegations to farms, pack-houses and loading stations and immediate issuance of certificate of origins and phyto-sanitary certificates.
  • PTI for all reefer trailers and constant monitoring by our professional drivers.
  • Door to Door Sea / Land reefer trailer services to Gulf Countries, UK and European destinations.
  • Voyage and time charters for ro-ro, ventilated and reefer vessels.
